## Product Summary

**InvoicePK** is a GST-compliant invoice generator for Pakistani freelancers and small agencies.

**Core value proposition:**
> "Generate FBR-compliant GST invoices in 2 minutes. No accountant needed."

**Problem it solves:**
- Pakistani freelancers manually create invoices in Word/Excel
- They get GST calculations wrong — especially WHT, zero-rated, and STRN requirements
- Wrong invoices cause client payment delays and FBR compliance issues
- Existing tools (Zoho, FreshBooks) are expensive and not built for Pakistani tax rules

---

## Target Audience

### Primary — Pakistani Freelancers
- Software developers, designers, content writers, consultants
- Earning PKR 50,000–500,000/month
- Mix of local and international clients
- Registered with FBR (have NTN) — may or may not have STRN
- Currently using Word, Excel, or Canva for invoices
- Active on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and Facebook freelancing groups

### Secondary — Small Agencies
- 2–10 person software houses or design agencies
- Need to manage multiple clients and invoice history
- Care about looking professional to corporate clients

### Not the Target (MVP)
- Large companies with accounting software (SAP, QuickBooks)
- Non-IT businesses (retail, manufacturing) — different tax rules
- International freelancers not dealing with PKR or FBR

## Pricing

All prices in PKR. Simple two-tier model for MVP.

| Plan | Price | Invoice Limit | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | PKR 0/month | 5 invoices/month | GST calculation, PDF download, 1 business profile, 10 clients |
| **Pro** | PKR 999/month | Unlimited | Everything in Free + unlimited invoices, logo upload, PDF save to cloud, dashboard stats |

**Why PKR 999:**
- Under the PKR 1,000 psychological threshold
- Equivalent to ~USD 3.50 — affordable for anyone earning USD 500+/month
- One invoice paid = subscription paid for 3 months at a typical freelancer rate

**Free tier strategy:**
- 5 invoices/month is enough to test the product and get value
- Hits the limit right when a freelancer is busy — creates natural upgrade pressure
- Free tier users are marketing — every PDF they send shows "Generated by InvoicePK"

## Landing Page Requirements

The `/` landing page must include these sections in this order:

1. **Hero** — headline, subheadline, CTA button ("Create Free Invoice")
2. **Problem** — "Still making invoices in Word?" — 3 pain points
3. **Solution** — 3 core features with icons (GST auto-calc, PDF download, FBR-compliant)
4. **How It Works** — 3 steps: Create account → Fill invoice → Download PDF
5. **Sample PDF** — screenshot of a real InvoicePK invoice PDF
6. **Pricing** — Free vs Pro table in PKR
7. **FAQ** — 5 common questions (see below)
8. **Footer** — links, "Made in Pakistan 🇵🇰"

**FAQ content:**
- *Is InvoicePK free?* — Yes, up to 5 invoices per month. Pro is PKR 999/month for unlimited.
- *Is it FBR compliant?* — Yes. GST at 17%, zero-rated for IT exports, WHT support, NTN and STRN on PDF.
- *Do I need an STRN?* — Only if you charge GST. You can use InvoicePK without an STRN for exempt or zero-rated invoices.
- *Can I bill in USD?* — Yes. Each invoice can be PKR or USD.
- *Is my data safe?* — Yes. Your data is stored securely and never shared.
